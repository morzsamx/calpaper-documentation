Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

CalPaper (“the App”) is developed and operated by Balázs Huszár (“we”, “us”, “our”). This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles personal data when you use it.

1. Overview

CalPaper is a desktop application that connects to user-provided calendars and displays calendar events as a visual background.

The App is designed to process your data locally on your device. We do not collect or transmit your personal data to external servers.

2. Data We Access

When you connect a calendar, the App may access the following data:

  • Event titles
  • Event start and end times
  • Event descriptions
  • Event locations
  • Other event-related metadata available through the calendar provider

Supported calendar sources include:

  • Google Calendar
  • Microsoft Outlook Calendar
  • Apple Calendar
  • ICS/iCalendar calendar sources

3. How We Use the Data

The accessed data is used solely to:

  • Display your calendar events visually within the App
  • Generate background images based on your calendar content
  • Provide the core functionality of the App

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, analytics, or tracking.

4. Data Storage

All data processing happens locally on your device.

The App may store the following locally:

  • Cached calendar event data
  • User preferences and settings
  • Authentication tokens for connected services

This local storage is used only to ensure proper functionality, improve performance, and keep your connected calendars available in the App.

5. Data Sharing

We do not:

  • Transmit your personal data to our servers
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Sell or rent your data

Your calendar data remains under your control on your device.

6. Authentication and External Services

To connect calendars, the App uses:

  • OAuth authentication for Google and Microsoft accounts
  • App-specific passwords for Apple Calendar access
  • Direct access for ICS/iCalendar sources

Authentication is handled between your device and the relevant calendar provider. We do not have access to your account password.

7. Legal Basis for Processing

For users in the European Economic Area, the legal basis for processing personal data under the GDPR is:

  • Performance of a contract — processing necessary to provide the App’s calendar display functionality.
  • Legitimate interest — processing necessary for local App performance, caching, and user-selected functionality.

8. Data Retention

Data is retained only on your device and only as long as necessary for the App to function.

You can remove your data at any time by:

  • Disconnecting calendar accounts in the App
  • Clearing the App’s local data
  • Uninstalling the App

9. Your Rights

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have rights under GDPR, including:

  • The right to access your data
  • The right to correct inaccurate data
  • The right to erase your data
  • The right to restrict processing
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to object to processing

Since we do not store your data externally, these rights can usually be exercised by managing your data directly on your device or through your calendar provider.

10. Children’s Privacy

CalPaper is available to users of all age groups. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children, as no data is transmitted or stored outside the user’s device.

11. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data by:

  • Processing calendar data locally on your device
  • Avoiding external transmission of calendar data
  • Using secure authentication methods provided by calendar services

The security of your data also depends on the security of your device and your connected calendar accounts.

12. Third-Party Services

CalPaper does not use analytics services, crash reporting tools, advertising networks, or third-party tracking services.

13. Payments and Subscriptions

The App offers optional premium features through subscriptions handled exclusively by the Microsoft Store.

We do not process, store, or have access to your payment information. Billing and subscription management are handled by Microsoft.

14. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be published on this website: https://morzsamx.github.io/calpaper-documentation/
