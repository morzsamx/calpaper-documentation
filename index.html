FAQ

Which calendar services are supported?

CalPaper supports Google Calendar, Microsoft Outlook Calendar, Apple Calendar (via CalDAV), and standard ICS calendar feeds.

How do I connect my Google Calendar?

You can simply sign in with your Google account through Google's secure OAuth authentication flow and grant CalPaper read-only access to your calendar data.

How do I connect my Apple Calendar?

Apple Calendar can be connected using CalDAV with an Apple app-specific password.
Follow our step-by-step setup guide: Detailed Apple Calendar connection steps

Alternatively, you can share your Apple Calendar as a public ICS calendar and add it to CalPaper.

How do I connect my Outlook Calendar?

You can simply sign in with your Microsoft account through Microsoft's secure OAuth authentication flow and grant CalPaper read-only access to your calendar data.

How do I connect my ICS Calendar?

You can add your ICS calendar feed directly to CalPaper by providing the URL of your calendar's ICS file.

Can CalPaper edit my calendar data?

No. CalPaper only downloads calendar data for display purposes and does not have write access to your calendars.

Where is my calendar data stored?

Your calendar data stays on your device. CalPaper only stores locally cached data needed for displaying your calendars.

Do you collect or transmit personal data?

No. CalPaper does not send your calendar data to external servers. All data stays on your device and is only used locally for displaying your calendars.

Does CalPaper require an internet connection?

CalPaper can run without an internet connection, but calendar data and weather information cannot be refreshed while offline.

Does CalPaper support multiple monitors?

Yes. CalPaper works across multiple monitors. The Free plan uses the same layout on all displays, while the Premium plan supports independent per-display settings.

Can I customize themes and layouts?

Advanced theme and layout customization features are available with the Premium plan.

Does CalPaper start automatically with Windows?

Yes. CalPaper can start automatically when Windows launches.

What’s included in the Premium subscription?

The Premium plan unlocks unlimited calendar sources, faster auto-updates, premium themes, advanced customization options, the weather widget, and per-display settings.

Is there a free trial for the Premium subscription?

Yes. The Premium subscription includes a 1-week free trial. After the trial period ends, the subscription will be billed automatically unless canceled beforehand. You can cancel anytime during the trial at no cost.

Can I cancel my Premium subscription anytime?

Yes. Subscriptions are managed through your Microsoft account and can be canceled there anytime.
