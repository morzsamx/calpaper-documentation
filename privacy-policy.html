Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 29, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Calpaper collects only the minimum information required to provide core app functionality. Depending on your app implementation, this may include preferences, theme settings, and anonymized diagnostics.

2. How We Use Information

Collected data is used to:

  • Deliver and improve app features.
  • Save user configuration and personalization.
  • Monitor stability and fix bugs.

3. Data Storage

User preferences are typically stored locally on the device unless cloud sync is explicitly enabled. If remote services are used, data is transmitted over encrypted connections.

4. Third-Party Services

Calpaper may rely on third-party infrastructure providers for analytics or hosting. These providers process data according to their own privacy policies.

5. Your Rights

Users can request access, correction, or deletion of personal data where applicable by law.

6. Contact

For privacy requests, contact: privacy@calpaper.app
