Connect Apple Calendar to CalPaper

Follow these steps to connect your Apple Calendar account securely.

Step-by-step

  1. Open Apple ID settings at account.apple.com.
  2. Sign in, then go to Sign-In and Security.
  3. Create an App-Specific Password for CalPaper.
  4. Copy the generated password and keep it ready.
  5. Open CalPaper and go to calendar connections.
  6. Select Apple Calendar.
  7. Enter your Apple ID email and the app-specific password.
  8. Choose which calendars to sync, then confirm.

Troubleshooting

  • If login fails, regenerate the app-specific password and try again.
  • Ensure two-factor authentication is enabled on your Apple account.
  • Check your internet connection and Apple service status.
