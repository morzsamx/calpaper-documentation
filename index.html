Stay on top of your schedule without even trying.

Calendar widgets on phones are easy to ignore, and they rarely give you a clear view of what’s ahead. CalPaper changes that by turning your calendar into your desktop background.

By displaying your connected calendars directly on your computer screen, your upcoming events are always visible—right where your attention already is. See your day, week, and plans at a glance on a large screen, without opening a single app.
